G.B. Road To Be Renamed G.S.T. Road

Oct 1, 2017 • english, India, political, lifestyle, shorts • 1 min read

“Aisi chudai kabhi na kiya aur na hi dekha”, said popular vendor Miss Bubbly (name changed to protect identity).

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) reform hangs as a swollen penis inside the pants double-edged sword over the heads of the entrepreneurs of Delhi’s infamous red light district – G.B. Road. With the services being offered in the area finally coming under the special services bracket of the tax, the life and work of the artists in the area has finally been legalized. However, the services are taxed at a giggling 69% which has left the employees complaining.

“I have to say, it hurts but thoda toh mazaa aa gaya”, said a proprietor.

“Main toh abhi condom bhi nahi khareed pata hai so main safed panni aur rubberband chadha ke hi kaam chala raha hai”, said another.
